About Us

The Research India Foundation is a not-for-profit organization dedicated to imparting quality research training and publication services to researchers in India. It aims to democratize research excellence by enabling researchers to acquire research knowledge, learn research skills, and conduct quality research as per the standards of international research and publication. The Foundation aims to cater to the research training and publication needs of its membersâ€”Faculty members, Research scholars, and UG/PG students of any discipline interested in learning how to do research. Research India Foundation accepts members from Basics Sciences, Engineering and Technology, Medical Sciences; AYUSH (Ayurveda, Yoga & Naturopathy, Unani, Siddha and Homoeopathy), Social Sciences, Management Sciences, and Humanities. The Foundation is committed to advancing research excellence in disciplinary, interdisciplinary and multidisciplinary research in all subjects. The Research India Foundation comes into effect on 1st of January 2021.

Vision and Mission

Our Vision

Research India Foundation aims to emerge as the largest dependable forum for accessible and affordable research training and development to all researchers in India who wish to gain research knowledge and develop/enhance their research skills for quality research and high impact publication

Our Mission

Research India Foundation aims to impart research knowledge and training to its members with the following objectives and USPs.

Mission Objectives

  • 01 Research Knowledge Dissemination

    Research India Foundation will make available to its members useful research information every month on:
    1. Current research funding proposals invited by national and international funding agencies (both government and private);
    2. Information on Useful books on Research Methodology;
    3. Information on Special issues of journals inviting papers;
    4. Information on Call for Books, Book chapters, and Monographs by reputed publishing houses;
    5. Information on the forthcoming Conferences, Seminars, Workshops, UGC Refresher courses, Training schedules;
    6. Information on Doctoral, Post-Doctoral and other Fellowships from Universities and organizations across the world (e.g., Fullbright Scholarship); and
    7. Information on Free International Professional Body Membership

  • 02 Research Training & Development

    Research India Foundation will organize and conduct Conferences, FDPs, Webinars, Workshops, Seminars for imparting research knowledge and skills to its bona fide members.

  • 03 Personalized Attention to Each Member

    Research India Foundation will help each of its members by personalized attention on their research needs for training and publication as per their need

  • 04 PhD Research Support From Beginning Till End

    Research India Foundation will support its members in their research journey, especially those who are doing PhD or intend to do PhD in any discipline as per their personal needs, by training the interested member to (1) write Research proposal, (2) take Mock Viva for PhD admission, (3) receive personalized unofficial guidance in PhD thesis writing from beginning till submission of thesis or at any stage of PhD journey, and (4) take Mock Viva Voce for defending PhD thesis. Eminent Research Consultants with remarkable experience and expertise will be made available to both aspiring and incumbent PhD Research Scholars for personalized counselling and guidance

  • 05 High-impact Journal Paper Writing Support

    Research India Foundation will support members write research papers for publication in high-impact international journals indexed in SCI, SCOPUS, ABDC-listed and UGC CARE-listed journals.

  • 06 Book Publication

    Research India Foundation will help its members publish books by reputed international (mainly European) and national publishers.

  • 07 Consulting Editors for Books

    Research India Foundation will nominate interested and competent members to edit books, PhD thesis for book publication, etc. and such members will earn credits as Editor and amount as fixed by the Foundation.

  • 08 Promoting Foundation Members

    Research India Foundation will publish landmark achievements of its members on the Research India Magazine with their photographs. Besides, the Foundation will recommend its members to different organizations for awards

  • 09Awards

    Research India Foundation will confer Research Excellence Award to its outstanding researchers every year on its Foundation Day (1st of January).

  • 10 Seed Funding

    Research India Foundation will try to arrange donors for researchers who have compelling need to buy equipment or for setting up libraries for research and development (R&D) facilities.

  • 11 Earning Scheme

    Research India Foundation will help each member earn money to fund their own research work in exchange of services rendered to the Foundation. Any member can earn up to Rs 20, 000/- to Rs 50, 000/- per month as Acquisition Editor for book publication of interested authors/researchers in any subject (Read the Centres & Activities section for the details). Members who will be doing deplagiarization (our term for removing plagiarism) from manuscripts of PhD thesis, journal research papers, and other publishable documents will be able to earn from these pursuits.

  • 12 Research Collaboration

    Research India Foundation will help different members of the Foundation to collaborate disciplinary, interdisciplinary, and transdisciplinary research.

  • 13 Patent Drafting and Filing

    Research India Foundation will help its members in drafting and filing patents in India, Europe, & America

  • 14 Anti-Plagiarism Support

    Research India Foundation will help its members to be aware of the issues involving plagiarism and help them in removing plagiarism in their PhD thesis and research papers.

  • 15 Research Software Support

    Research India Foundation will help its members learn about different software for their research work and how to use them.

  • 16 Content Editing & English Language Editing Support

    Research India Foundation will help its members to get their contents edited and improve the language of their PhD thesis and research papers as per international Native speakersâ€™ standards

  • 17 Online Courses

    Research India Foundation will help its members to offer courses as MOOCs.

  • 18 Converting PhD Thesis into Books

    Research India Foundation will help its members in converting their M.Tech./M.Phil/MBA dissertations into books/monographs by upgrading, editing, and developing for publication

  • 19 Research Consultancy

    Research India Foundation will allow well-experienced and expert researchers competent for guiding PhD scholars and Research Paper writers and earn honorarium for such services.

  • 20 Research Administration

    Research India Foundation will give its members opportunities to develop/utilize their research administration skills by working as research administrators in different capacities on the Executive Boards of the Centres of the Foundation.

  • 21 Right to Serve

    Research India Foundation is committed to giving opportunity to each competent member to serve on the Executive Board of the Foundation.
